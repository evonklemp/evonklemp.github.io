About:

I am writer and educator based in Brooklyn. I received an MFA in fiction from Columbia University, where I acted as the Columbia Journal's Online Fiction editor. My book reviews and author interviews have been featured in Tin House online, LAMBDA Literary, Electric Literature, The Rumpus, Full Stop, and more. I mentor for Girls Write Now in my spare time.
